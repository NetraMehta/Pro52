About CreativeOrigami


Our mission is to increase public enjoyment and understanding of origami which a a very special and beautiful form of art that dates back to the 17th Century Japan. It is still a very interesting craft form which anyone can make by just a small piece of paper.



Who We Are

CreateOrigami is an online platform that helps art lovers learn origami. It is a free organisation which has been working for the past Seven years now.

What We Do

We help people of all ages develop an interest into art by giving them step by step instructions on different shapes and things anyone could create using a simple piece of paper.

How We Work

We collaborate with non-charitable trusts to fund our organisation. We have over 10,000 people working from different parts of the world who keep us on track. If you have some other ideas about origami, you can always contact us.
